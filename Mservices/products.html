Our products

Through out all our years of experience in the cleaning business, we have found that not all cleaning products can get the job done.
To help our customers be more informed of how we take care of their homes and feel confident that we are using the best products on the market, we share with you the products we use in every of our cleaning appointments.

Meyers Multi-surface

The most used product to clean every surface.
Meyers is a quick and easy multi-surface spray that removes dirt and dust from surfaces throughout the house and leaves a fresh scent.



Sprayway Glass Cleaner

Not all glass cleaners are made the same. We use Sprayway Glass cleaner.
Its foaming action makes it excellent for windows, mirrors, windshields and other glass surfaces.



Simple Green All purpose cleaner

The surface that gets the dirtiest the fastest is the floor. Simple Green is and all-purpose concentrated cleaner that removes dirt, grease, and stains. We can use it for heavy messes, or easily dilute with water for daily cleaning.



Bona Hardwood Floor cleaner

Bona Hardwood Floor cleaner is a residue-free, fast drying solution that cleans hardwood floors by gently removing dust, dirt and grime to reveal the floor's natural shine and beauty. Is a Waterbased solution to ensure a safer product for people, pets, and the planet.



Zep Industrial cleaner and degreaser

One of the places that grease gets stubornly stuck in the kitchen.
For this we use Zep Industial Cleaner and Degreaser. A strong degreaser that just needs seconds to act in that oven grease to leave its clean surfaces looking like new
